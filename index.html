About our Games

Trivia Game:

A multiple choice trivia game. Read the question then click the card to find the answer. Play vs friends or in teams or even test your knowlege solo. Play to first to 5 correct or 10 or however many you want! Even race to see who gets the answer first, lots of options and lots of questions! There's over 75 questions currently and plenty more to come!

Drinking Game:

A perfect card game to play with friends. Gather your friends and your drinks and get ready for good laughs and a good buzz. For players 21+. This card game is still in development and is expected to be released soon!!

Small Talk:

On a long car ride, plane ride or just sitting around a table with friends. Hit the play now button and start discussing and learn more about each other or just have a good time with funny questions!! New questions added weekly.

What's in future development???

The future development consists of adding new triva questions weekly as well as more small talk cards weekly. For future games, a hangman is in the future as well as well as a random joke/random facts generator incase you need a good laugh each day or learn something new!
